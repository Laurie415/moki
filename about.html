About Us

MOKI Entertainment
Is dedicated to its primary goal of creating diverse projects with multi-ethnic casts. Moki's targeted effort is to write and/or seek, find, option, develop, and produce quality films for "Movie of the Week", the "Silver Screen", DVD direct distribution, feature option pictures production.

LEI MOWATT
Founded MOKI Entertainment Inc. as a production company in January of 2004 to develop and produce projects and fresh ideas in Film and Television Production. As an accomplished Writer / Producer, he has worked closely with Writer / Director / Producer Kantz to complete a Sci-Fi Horror feature.

His numerous works include:

  • Lessons Learned: Teen Dating, a teen educational pilot that is currently being presented to appropriate television stations and cable companies for distribution.
  • Project Purgatory, a post-apocolyptic science fiction horror about the last humans on the planet that live their fairly normal lives by day, only to fight for continued existance at night when constantly attacked by forever adapting, predatorial radiation victims. Maverick Entertainment Group has agreed to distribute Project Purgatoryworldwide with a release date scheduled for early 2014.
  • Repeat Offenders, a film about three teams that keep reliving the same vicious day over and over again in diferent time lines is in post-production.
  • Forbidden, a fairytale love story between a modern-day warrior and the wealthy heiress he is bound to protect.
  • The Last Wish, how three friends learn about morals and each other with the help of a genie.
  • Retirement Club, a place for people with time to kill.
  • Freedom in the Mind's Eye,a psychological hunt to save a woman's soul.
  • Mercenary Assault, Mercenaries that take on terrorism and organized crime to save Americans.
  • Committed, a faith-based, modern-day look at how the trail of Jesus Christ might have taken place in todays world as seen through the eyes of one individual who questions their own faith.
  • The Messengers, an action-drama about one family that has reached their all time low and how their newfound neighbors work miracles to them.
